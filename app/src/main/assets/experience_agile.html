AGILE

On the go

It has been fun to work in an Agile development setting for the past 8 years.

The benefits from such methodology involve adjusting course of action sooner, optimizing productivity and having more realistic deadlines.

Since all the parties get constant updates, management does have fair certainty of when the project will be completed.

Let's face it, we are humans and we do make mistakes both in planning and development, but either one can be corrected sooner when Agile is followed.

Of course, if all the phases of development have been carefully executed without Agile, the project may also have great outcomes.
