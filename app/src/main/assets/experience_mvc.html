MVC

Great architecture

I have been implementing MVC in almost all the projects I am involved with, unless I am required not to.

This truly such an amazing software architectural pattern for implementing user interfaces and having a better handle on the development.

It promotes team work and ease of code maintenance when properly implemented, to the point where developers are delighted to maintain one another's code.

When I look back at the many roads that led me all the way from procedural to MVC, it's makes me realize how much growth was involved in the process.

The knowledge and experience has continually changed my perspective as a professional.
