J-Pro Inspects - Android App

It's a Home Inspection business Android App, which provides comprehensive information about their business, terms and conditions, credibility, references and qualifications.

This App is solely based in Fragments, Webviews and some custom tabs.

The contact and quote forms communicate with a custom web service, which then stores the data in a private server and sends the business an email about it.
