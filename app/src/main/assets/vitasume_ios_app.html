Vitasume - iOS App

This app is allows users enroll their resumes and make them available to everyone. The best part is... NO ADS!

Users can enter different information about their resumes, such as Summary, Strengths, Professional Experience, Other Relevant Experience and Education.

The main screen allows the users to share their full name, phone number, email and social media link. All in this one app!

You can load anyone's resume by using their email address a well, making it the perfect resume sharing app.
